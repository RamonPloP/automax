About Us

Our Vision

To be the benchmark company and reach a leadership position in the market, maintaining volumes that provide adequate profitability for the continuity of the company. To be a Mexican company of reference in the automotive sector in terms of quality, technology, profitability and strength, exceeding the expectations of customers, employees and suppliers.


Mission

To be a company specialized in the commercialization of cars. To have highly qualified personnel, state-of-the-art technology and infrastructure that always guarantee an integral service of the highest quality. To comply with the standards of the brands, environmental norms and social environment and to work for the welfare and growth of our human talent. Our mission is geared towards excellence, that is to say, towards total customer satisfaction, as well as that of the professionals who are part of it, respectful environmental behavior and the distinction of leadership.


Values

AutoMax is currently inspired by and acts in accordance with the following corporate values:

  • Honesty
  • Commitment
  • Seriousness
  • Respect for people and the environment
  • Empathy
  • Responsibility
